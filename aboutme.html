Jennifer's Story - Always Improving and Growing

Jennifer has been in front of a computer since post high school, starting as a LAN Administrator. While she was fixing computers and supporting software, she finished her Bachelors Degree in Business.

Jennifer then changed careers and became the first female Remote Data Recovery Engineer in the world. While that was a rewarding responsibility, she decided she wanted to bring out the creative side of her skills as she always had an interest in design.

To grow and explore new opportunities, Jennifer became a freelance contractor and began building websites using best practices for responsive design and SEO. Always focusing on growth mindset, she enhanced her skillset by completing her UX/UI Design certificate.

In her free time, Jennifer volunteers to a local nonprofit that focuses on fishing education and adventures. On her days off, she loves to ski, camp, hike, travel, and most importantly spend time with her family.
