Designs are based on user research and then transformed into meaningful designs.

As a self-starter, Jennifer thrives on staying fluid with changing technology and continuing to grow and enhance her skills.

Jennifer's Story - Always Improving and Growing

Jennifer has been in front of a computer since post high school, starting as a LAN Administrator. While she was fixing computers and supporting software, she finished her Bachelors Degree in Business.

Jennifer then changed careers and became the first female Remote Data Recovery Engineer in the world. While that was an enormous responsibility, she decided she wanted to bring out the creative side of her skills as she always had an interest in design.

To grow and explore new opportunities, Jennifer became a freelance contractor and began building websites using best practices for responsive design and SEO. She is now enhancing her skillset by completing her UX/UI Design certificate. She is excited to put her refined skills to work.

“No one solution is the same, but turning ideas into meaningful experiences is my goal.”
